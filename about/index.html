About

Hi, I'm Sérgio Pereira but usually go by SergioEduP or TheSergioEduP online.

You can read my introduction on my blog post.1 - Hello, welcome and why?, but here is a shorter version:

I'm a maker from Portugal born in 2001 who has collected a lot of hobbies over the years such as:

  • Using Arch BTW (messing around with Linux)
  • Learning
  • Programming
  • 3D Modeling for 3D Printing
  • Sketching
  • Gaming
  • and even Making little Games
  • (and as a bonus I guess Blogging now)

Unfortunately I've also collected a bunch of anxiety and bad habits over the years by spending too much time on the same 5 websites, anxiety that lead me to not share much of what I make and deleting the little that I did share.

But during 2024 I've identified a lot of these bad habits and cues that trigger them and I decided to take action against them, to actually make things and share them since that is what makes me happy, this website is where I will do just that, and hopefully inspire some people to do the same.

I know I'm not the best at any of the things that I do but that won't stop me from trying and sharing what I end up making, doesn't matter if I succeed or fail, it is a learning process that will make me better at whatever I decide to try my hand at and will also serve as a learning process to anyone who might read my blog as they might learn a thing or two trough my failures and successes.

So if you decide to stay you are more than welcome to join me in this journey that I'm embarking on!
